Why Love Running With Us?

Feel Healthy

Since it improves aerobic fitness, running is a great way to help improve your cardiovasular health. Plus it burns calories and can build strength.

Feel strong

Running strengthens your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body

Feel free

Physical excercise, running included, is known to release endorphins in the brain. Endorphins make your more energetic, more away and, yes, happier.

Get social

When we run with others, we can run farther and faster more easily. Social running can pull you out of a running funk or low mood, and inspire you to try new events.
